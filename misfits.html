Misfits

Misfits follows five delinquents on community service who are caught outside during a supernatural thunder storm and who acquire special abilities. Initially, the show focused on five young adults, each gaining a superpower which mirrors their character. Kelly Bailey (Lauren Socha)-- constantly judged for her manly and brute appearance—gains the ability of telepathy, Curtis Donovan (Nathan Stewart-Jarrett)--trying to escape a mistake from his past—can rewind time after experiencing an immense sense of regret, Alisha Daniels (Antonia Thomas)-- a woman that is extremely comfortable with her sexuality and body—sends people into a sexual frenzy when they touch her skin and Simon Bellamy (Iwan Rheon)-- often ignored or not acknowledged—can become invisible. Nathan Young (Robert Sheehan) appears unchanged.
